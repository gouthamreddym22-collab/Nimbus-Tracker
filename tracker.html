Nimbus — Progress

Working title — product name TBD

Nimbus is a continuous personal AI companion — server-hosted, accessible via voice and chat — that develops a persistent relationship with each user over years.

Built on a five-verb framework — Serve, Anticipate, Guide, Safeguard, Represent — and powered by licensed frontier models (Claude, GPT, Gemini) orchestrated by a personal-identity layer Nimbus owns. One Nimbus per user. Deeply personalised. Not a chatbot in front of someone else's model.

This tracker measures honest progress toward Phase 1 closed alpha: 50–100 invited users on working SaaS infrastructure. Numbers are deliberately conservative.
